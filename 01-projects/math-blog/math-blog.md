- Category theory motivation (vs. set theory)
- Tensors (from multilinear maps to musical isomorphisms)

- Signal processing & Fourier transforms
- Inverse problems (beamforming)

- What it all means for quantitative science

## Unifying Thread: Opposite Categories

The duality/opposite-category thread runs through every post:
- Sets vs. Categories → families of functions as objects
- Tensors → covariant vs. contravariant (hints at opposite category)
- Duality → opposite categories as "reversing arrows"
- Measurement → functionals as elements of dual space
- Fourier/Beamforming → dual representation in frequency space
- Stochastic processes → policies as maps dual to system dynamics
- Learning → primal-dual optimization structure
